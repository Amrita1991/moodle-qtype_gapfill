Version 1.6 of the Moodle Gapfill question type contributed by Marcus Green
When fixed gapsize the width of a gap such as [cat|tiger] will be the width of tiger not cat|tiger, i.e. 5 not 9
When show answers is selected in the quiz the correct answer will be displayed within the question delimiters, so if 
the correct answer is [cat] and you enter[dog] the answer will show dog [cat] (with dog in red followed by a tick)
